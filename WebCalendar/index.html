Introduction

Welcome to the Wolverine Alley Calendar! You no longer have to wonder whether you've completed all your tasks for the day. Now, there's a calendar that will document every employees' daily tasks throughout the month. Let's begin the tutorial. If you want to see a quick snapshot of the calendar in action, click here: Calendar Snapshot . Note: The Wolverine Alley Calendar is intended for all staff. If you want your own personal calendar to manage your day to day responsibilities, try: Google Calendar

Navigation

To navigate between one month to another, click on one of the arrows in the upper corners of the screen. For the previous month, click on the arrow in the upper left. For the following month, click on the arrow in the upper right.

Day View

To view the tasks for a given day, click on the desired day located on the main calendar.

Tasks

A new window will appear that shows all the tasks scheduled for that day. These tasks are colored according to employee color IDs, and are in order of precedence.

Completing Tasks

Once a task has been completed, click on the circle next to the task, then click the red 'X' in the lower left-hand corner.

New Task

To add a new task to the current day, click on the blue plus icon near the top right corner.

Creating Task

In the new task window, enter the employee name, title of the task, the estimated time of completion (ETC), the precedence of the task, and the instructions on how to complete the task.

Save Task

Once all the details have been entered for the new task, click the green check mark in the lower right-hand corner.

Employee Key

In the upper left-hand corner of the main calendar view, you'll see an employee key.

Add Employee

To add an employee to the key, click on the add button, located at the bottom of the key.

New Employee

In the new employee window, enter the employees name, and their associated color name, which will be used to identify tasks assigned to that employee.

Save Employee

To save the new employee, click on the green checkmark in the lower right-hand corner. To cancel the new employee, click the red 'X' in the lower left hand corner.

Employees

Once a new employee has been added, they will appear in the employee key.
